Stacks & Queues

These are the next foundational tools for solving:
    - Expression parsing
    - Parentheses validation
    - Monotonic patterns
    - BFS (in graphs & trees)

------------------------------

Stack

LIFO: Last-In, First-Out

Java: Stack<>, Deque<>, or ArrayDeque<>

Used for:
- Undo operations
- Validating parentheses
- Tracking previous values

------------------------------


Stack is perfect for problems with nested structure or reverse matching

Use push for open brackets, and pop for matching close

Always check for empty stack before popping
